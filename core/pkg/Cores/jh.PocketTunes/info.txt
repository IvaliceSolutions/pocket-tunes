Pocket Tunes 0.1.0 (M2 bring-up)
--------------------------------
Music player core for Analogue Pocket. This build is the M2 hardware
bring-up: it boots, outputs 400x360 video (4x-scaled to the full LCD),
and loads /Assets/pockettunes/common/library.json generated by the
pocket-tunes-indexer tool, displaying its bytes and size on screen.

No audio playback yet. UI and MP3 decoding arrive in later milestones.
